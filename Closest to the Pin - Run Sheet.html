Closest to the Pin — Run Sheet

Two branded monday forms feed a custom big-screen board: people submit a structured entry (prompt + image), then vote from their phones, and the whole room watches it resolve on a TaylorMade screen. Built, validated end-to-end, and wired into the deck.

A The pieces (built & ready) no setup needed

  • Submit form — forms.monday.com/forms/bb491a7a9729b39e0d3bb91210f5f7ae. Guided fields: Name → Scenario → Your prompt → Upload image. TM-red, no login. Its QR is on the Submit Card and the deck's contest slide.
  • Big-screen board — Closest to the Pin - Board.html. Full-screen, on-brand; shows every entry grouped by the three pins, with images. Keys: V open voting · R reveal winners · G gallery · F fullscreen.
  • Vote page (live) — ctp-proxy.ali-chitsaz.workers.dev/vote. Lists the real entries; one tap per pin to vote. Its QR fills the board when you press V.

B Submit ~8 min, during the contest

  • Put the Submit Card on tables / show its QR. People scan → pick scenario → paste prompt → (headcover) attach image → Submit. Structured fields, no login.
  • Regionals can write their prompt in their own language — Claude handles JP / KO / ES, and it doubles as a live localization demo.
  • Each submission lands as a monday item with the prompt and the image together — that's what the board reads.

C Reveal & vote on the big screen

  • The board refreshes itself — it pulls live from monday through the Cloudflare Worker every ~12 s and soft-updates in place (no reload, no flicker), so new shots and votes just appear. Nothing to keep open; the Worker runs on its own.
  • Open the board (L in the deck, or the ⛳ Board button). Walk each pin so the room sees the shots — read a couple of prompts aloud.
  • Press V → the Vote QR fills the screen. The room scans it to open the live vote page, which already lists the real entries — one tap per pin. Votes tally on the board in real time.
  • Press R → reveal: the bars animate, the top shot in each pin is crowned. Three champions. Tie or a runaway? You're the rules official.
No pre-event setup — the board and vote page read live from monday through the Cloudflare Worker (always on). Voting is honor-system: one tap per pin, with a soft per-phone lock (no hard one-per-person without logins).

D Say this

Open: “Three pins, three scenarios. You write the prompt — that's the whole game. Best shot in the room, judged by the room.”

Submit: “Scan, pick your pin, paste your prompt, attach your image, submit. Ninety seconds.”

Vote: “Everyone's shots are on the screen. Scan, pick your favorite in each pin. Vote for the shot, not your buddy.”

Engine: monday.com forms + boards (submissions board 18418880107, votes board 18418881836), in the TaylorMade workspace. Big screen: a self-contained TaylorMade HTML page that reads those entries. Image links are monday signed URLs (valid ~1 hour) — the board is regenerated just before the reveal.
